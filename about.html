About RDP Management System

Your Path to Affordable Housing – Empowering citizens through access to government-funded housing programs.

The Housing Crisis

  • Rising Homelessness: Affects millions due to lack of affordable housing.
  • Skyrocketing Rent: Rent prices are outpacing incomes globally.
  • Limited Housing Inventory: Insufficient supply leads to competition and displacement.

Why RDP Management?

  • Affordable Housing: Government-subsidized options reduce cost burden.
  • Simplified Process: One platform. Fewer forms. Faster decisions.
  • Empowerment: Find housing, regain stability, and plan your future.

Our Users

We serve individuals, families, and seniors (18+) with low to moderate income in both urban and rural areas.

Our Vision

To become South Africa’s leading digital gateway for accessing government housing support through transparency, innovation, and user-first design.

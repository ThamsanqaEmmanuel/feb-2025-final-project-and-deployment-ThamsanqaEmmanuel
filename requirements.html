RDP Application Requirements


Basic Eligibility

✓ South African citizen
✓ Over 18 years old
✓ Married/cohabiting or single with dependents
✓ Household income under R3,500.01/month
✓ First-time subsidy & home owner

Documents Needed

✓ South African ID
✓ Birth certificates of dependents
✓ Proof of income (or affidavit if unemployed)

How to Apply

✓ Visit your local municipality
✓ Submit application with documents
✓ Wait (around 2 years)
✓ Receive house and "Happy Letter"
✓ Title Deed issued after 8 years

Important Notes

⚠ No sale within 8 years
⚠ Houses are completely free
⚠ Selling early only allowed back to the government
